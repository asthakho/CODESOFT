What is this project?

The Iris dataset contains measurements of 150 flowers across 3 species — Setosa, Versicolor, and Virginica. We trained 4 machine learning models to classify flowers based on their sepal and petal measurements. All 4 models achieved 100% accuracy on the test set.

4 Features Used
📏 Sepal Length (cm)
📐 Sepal Width (cm)
🌿 Petal Length (cm)
🌱 Petal Width (cm)
Models Tested
🔵 K-Nearest Neighbors
🌳 Decision Tree
🌲 Random Forest
